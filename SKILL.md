## Quick facts
- **Brand color**: GaleON green `#9ACA3C` on ink `#1f2a0c`.
- **Type**: Plus Jakarta Sans, tight tracking on display, `tabular-nums` + `ss01/ss02` on stats.
- **Neutrals**: warm — off-white bg `#F7F7F4`, fg `#0E0F0E`, muted `#6B6B68`.
- **Service palette**: 7 colors, one per offering (see `colors_and_type.css`). Color follows the service, not aesthetics.
- **No gradients as decoration** — only as legibility scrims over photography.
- **No emoji**, no multi-color icons. Iconography is hand-rolled 24×24 line SVG, stroke 1.6.
- **Photography is the primary visual.** Pair every photo with a scrim when copy sits on top.
- **Voice**: editorial, calm, oficial — closer to an institutional travel publication than a delivery app.
